About

In today's tech-driven world, mastering technology is crucial. We propose the Axios Technical Club to foster innovation and skill development. This club will enhance students' technical abilities, promote collaboration, and prepare them for industry challenges.

VISION & MISSION

Our mission is to create a student community provides exposure and opportunities to build digital footprints and apply problem-solving skills to real-life challenges.

OBJECTIVES

  • - Workshops, Seminars and Webinars
  • - Project Based Learning
  • - Internship Assurance
  • - Hands-On Projects
  • - Open Source Projects
  • - Coding Culture
  • - Digital Footprints
  • - Collaborative Learning & Work Experience
  • - Industry Ready Professionals

BENEFITS TO STUDENTS
